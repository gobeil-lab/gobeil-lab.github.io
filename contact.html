Get in touch!

Are you a student interested in structural biology (X-ray crystallography, Cryo-EM and NMR), biomedicine and protein engineering? If so, consider joining the team! We’re always on the lookout for motivated and passionate people (and we are bilingual!).

Students: We are recruiting students at all level (Undergrad, M.Sc. and Ph.D.). The lab is currently setting up - This is a great opportunity to learn the ropes! If you’re interested in working with us, just send an email!

Postdoctoral Fellows: There may be opportunities for a funded postdoc position in the future once the lab is established. However, if you have your own funding or are looking to apply for a fellowship and would like to join our team, send us an email!

Living in Québec City: Québec is an absolutely beautiful city with lots of European charm. It is also a "foodies city" (come enjoy some of the best Poutine!) with an exceptionally fun and blooming craft beer scenery. Nature is also not far away: whether it's for hiking, skiing or simply enjoying the colors of fall, apple picking or the sugar shack, you'll have plenty of time to enjoy the pleasures that Québec has to offer! There’s always activities and festivals to attend.

Come for the passion of research, enjoy the culture, and nature!
